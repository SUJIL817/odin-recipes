Easy Chicken Piccata Orzo Casserole

Home

Description

This easy chicken piccata orzo casserole is a one-dish dinner with lemon-garlic chicken atop creamy orzo. It takes minutes to assemble, and is on the table in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • 8 ounces orzo
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast, sliced into cutlets
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
